FAQ's Line Listing Reports

What are line-listing reports?

Line-listing reports are set of reports which report those beneficiaries and Ashas on whom specific action needs to be taken. For example, in case a pregnant mother is not listening to Kilkari messages continuously, she needs to be educated on the importance of the service and encouraged to listen the full content.

What is the follow-up activity that needs to be performed on the line-listing reports?

Each line-listing report is emailed to your email ID once it is generated along with the instructions that need to be followed for the report. Please check your email on the next steps that need to be followed. For any further clarifications, share your queries through the contact us form.
