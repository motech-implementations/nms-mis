FAQ

The FAQ page contains answers to frequently asked questions about the application.

1) The FAQ page can be accessed by clicking on the FAQ tab:



2) In the FAQ page, the General Information is the default selected tab.

3) Clicking on any of the tabs in the left pane shows the respective section.

4) Each section has relevant frequently asked questions.

5) Clicking on a question shows the answer to that question.
