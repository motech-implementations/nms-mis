Beneficiary listen to 25%


1) Under "Reports Category" select "Kilkari Reports", then





2) Under "Reports" select "Listen to <25% this month"





3) Then select the Month





4) Then select respective State, District and Block




5) To view the result, press the "Select" button, or to reset it, press "Reset" button



6) Click on the download link to download the report. All the reports will be downloaded as Excel files.

7) Clicking on ‘Reset’ button will reset all the fields to their default values.

Deactivation for not answering

This report lists out all the Kilkari beneficiaries who were deactivated in the selected month, for not answering a call for 6 weeks continuously.

Steps to download the reports

1) select "Kilkari Reports" under "Reports Category"




2) Then select "Deactivated for not answering" under "Reports"





3) Then select the Month of which the reports are needed





4) Then select respective State, District and Block




5) To download the result, press the "Select" button



6) Click on the download link to download the report. All the reports will be downloaded as Excel files.

7) Clicking on ‘Reset’ button will reset all the fields to their default values.
