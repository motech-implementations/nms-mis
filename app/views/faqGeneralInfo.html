FAQ's General Information

How to get the user manual for the reports?

Click on "User Manual" in top navigation bar. Select a user role and click on a report category in the left navigation menu. Select a report from the category in the new menu shown.

MIS interface is supported by which all browsers and versions?

MIS interface is compatible with the following browsers and versions:

  • Google Chrome: version 42 and above
  • Mozilla Firefox: version 23 and above
  • Microsoft Internet Explorer: version 10 and above
  • Microsoft Edge: version 13 and above
  • Safari: version 7 and above
  • Opera: version 29 and above
  • UC Browser: version 7
What is the minimum internet speed I need on my computer to be able to access the MIS?

The minimum internet speed required to access the MIS portal is 256 kbps.

I’m trying to download a report or log in but the page is continuously loading!

While downloading a report: If the report is already generated you will get it instantly. If it is not already present it will take time until it is generated.

While Login: This might be because of your internet speed.

I’m trying to download a report or log in but it is giving 404 error, time out error or any other error, what to do?

In case of 404 error the page you are trying to open might not exist, so please check the url and try again. In case of any other error, try reloading the page and if the error persists please reach out to us through the "Contact Us" form.

I’m not receiving the line listing reports on my email?

If you are a registered user who is at district or block level you will get the line listing reports via email on the registered email address. In case of user at state level district wise counts of corresponding reports are sent. Please check your registered email address for any discrepancies.

How can I change my email address on which the reports are being emailed?

You can update it from the "My profile" page or by contacting the admin who registered you to the application.
