Profile

1) The users can login into the system using the URL: https://rchivrreports.in/app/.

2) To login User must enter a valid Username and Password combination

The user must be sure to check the Captcha tickbox and fulfil any pop-up condition to be able to login successfully.

3) Then click on ‘Login’ button to log in to the MIS interface:




4) To logout from the application, click on the Profile name, located on the top-right as shown in above screen, then a dropdown will appear.




5)Then click on ‘Logout’ button




6) If you wish to change your password, click on ‘Change Password’ located above the logout option. Mention your old Password once and new password twice and then press 'Save' button.

A successful change will force a logout for the user to login again.

In case you press 'Cancel', you can continue with the same session, but will remain in the 'Change Password' screen.




7) Click on 'My Profile' located above the change password option to view your profile.




8) To update your email-id or contact-no., write your new email or contact number in the respective input boxes and click on Update Contact Info button.




Before clicking 'Update Contact Info' Button, to change your email-id and contact number to previous one, click on Reset button.


9) To request a change in any other details, the user will have to contact the admin who created their account.
