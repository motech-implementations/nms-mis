Profile

1) The users can login into the system using the URL: https://rchivrreports.in/app/.

2) To login User must enter a valid User Name and Password combination

3) Then click on ‘Login’ button to log in to the MIS interface




4) To logout from the application, click on the Profile name, located on the top-right as shown in above screen, then a dropdown will appear.




5)Then click on ‘Logout’ button




6) Click on ‘Change Password’ located above the logout option. Mention your old Password once and new password twice and then press change password button




7) Click on 'My Profile' located above the change password option to view your profile.

To update your email-id or contact-no., write your new email or contact number in the respective input boxes and click on Update Contact Info button.

Before clicking 'Update Contact Info' Button, to change your email-id and contact number to previous one, click on Reset button.
