Profile

1) The users can login into the system using the URL: https://rchivrreports.in/.



2) Enter the valid User Name and Password combination, check the Captcha tickbox and click on ‘Login’ button to log in to the MIS interface. Following screen appears on successful login.





3) For profile details, click on the Profile name, located on the top-right as shown in above screen, then a dropdown will appear.




4) Click on 'My Profile' in the dropdown to view your profile.




5) To update your email-id or contact-no., enter new email-id or contact number in the respective input boxes and click on Update Contact Info button.




6) Before clicking 'Update Contact Info' Button, to revert changes, click on Reset button.



7) To change any other details, contact the admin who created the account.

8) To change password, click on ‘Change Password’ in the profile dropdown. Fill all the required fields and click on 'Save' button.



9) After successful password change, application navigates to login page.


10) To logout, click on logout button in the profile dropdown.
