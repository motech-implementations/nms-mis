FAQ's Login and Registration

How do I register to the MIS?

Please contact your regional administrator/head/NHM. They will create a user account for you. The following are the access levels supported in the system:

  • National level: Can access reports for all the states where the services are launched.
  • State level: Can access reports for all regions within the state.
  • District level: Can access reports for the district and regions within the district.
  • Block level: Can access reports for the corresponding health block.
Who creates the administrators and users of the MIS?

The admins are at National, State & district level as follows:

  • National Admin: The national level administrator can create national level users and state level administrators
  • State level Admin: A state level administrator can create state level users and district level administrators
  • District level Admin: A district level administrator can create district level users and block level users. There are no administrators below the district level
Who is an administrator?

Administrators are users who can administer and track other users. They can administer users only within their location. They can do so through user management tab, which shows up once they login. They can create users, reset their password and disable access to users. There will be only one administrator for each state and district.

How do I login for the first time?

The regional administrator/head/NHM will provide you with your user ID. Your phone number is the default password. After first login, you will be provided with an option to reset the password.

What do I do in case I forgot my password?

There is a forgot password feature on the homepage that you can use to reset password. If you are not sure of your phone number, please reach out to your administrator.

What do I do in case I forgot my User ID?

Please reach out to your administrator who has provided you the access.
